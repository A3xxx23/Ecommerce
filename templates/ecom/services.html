Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my order to arrive?

Domestic shipments usually take between 2-3 business days. For international shipments, the delivery time is 5-7 business days.

What is the shipping cost? ?

Shipping is free for orders over €50. For smaller orders, the cost is €4.95.

What is Return policy?

You have 30 days to return your order. The product must be unused and in its original packaging.

How can I make a size change?

You can request a size change from your account or by contacting customer service. The exchange is free.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer.

Do you offer payment in installments?

Yes, we offer interest-free financing in 3 installments for orders over €100.

How can I find my correct size?

Check our size guide on each product. You can also contact our customer service for personalized advice.

Are the products original?

Yes, all our products are 100% original and have a manufacturer's warranty.
